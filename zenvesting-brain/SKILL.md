# Zenvesting Brain: Transcript Processor

## Goal
Transform raw transcripts into high-quality, persona-driven Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

## Core Personas
- **Khan (Strategic Investor):** Logical, structural, long-term focus. 🏗️
- **Zara (High-Growth Specialist):** Enthusiastic, momentum-driven, engaging. 🔥

## Workflow
1.  **Analyze Transcript:** Identify key educational takeaways and strategic decision points.
2.  **Generate MCQs:** Create 5-10 questions with 4 options each.
3.  **Craft Explanations:** Every question must have feedback from both Khan and Zara.
4.  **JSON Validation:** Ensure output strictly follows the schema in `generator.py`.

## Persona Guidelines
- Khan's feedback should explain the "Why" behind the fundamentals.
- Zara's feedback should highlight the "Excitement" and growth potential.
